# Affiliate Attribution MVP

Status: not built

## Why It Matters

Affiliate behavior should be a scoped Connection capability with evidence, not
a separate marketing island.

## MVP Scope

- Mark a Connection as affiliate-capable.
- Issue referral link, QR code, and approved share copy.
- Track visits, sessions, offer acceptance, trial start, conversion, and credit
  review.
- Provide a minimal affiliate dashboard scoped to that affiliate's attribution.
- Let owner approve, pay, void, or dispute credit according to evidence.

## Durable Product Nouns

- Affiliate Connection
- Referral Asset
- Attribution Ledger
- Credit State
- Affiliate Dashboard View

## Acceptance Criteria

- Affiliate only sees scoped attribution evidence.
- Credit cannot be granted without evidence.
- Owner can review and decide credit state.
- Attribution survives from entry point through trial/conversion.

## Non-Goals

- Payment automation.
- Marketplace.
- Cross-Ordo affiliate network.

## Validation

- Access policy tests.
- Attribution state transition tests.
- UI smoke for scoped dashboard once built.
